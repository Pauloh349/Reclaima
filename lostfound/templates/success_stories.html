Featured Story: A Grandmother's Necklace Returns Home

Jessica Williams
October 5, 2023
Jewelry

When Jessica moved apartments, she lost her grandmother's necklace - a family heirloom passed down through three generations. After weeks of searching and losing hope, she decided to post on Reclaima as a last resort.

"I had given up all hope of ever seeing that necklace again. It wasn't just jewelry to me - it was a connection to my grandmother and the stories she used to tell."

Two months later, a construction worker found the necklace at a nearby demolition site and posted it on Reclaima. The unique description of the pendant's engraving made it easy for Jessica to identify as hers.

"When I got the notification that my necklace might have been found, I couldn't believe it. Meeting the person who found it was emotional - we both cried. Reclaima didn't just return my necklace; it restored my faith in people."
